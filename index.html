About Halziz

About Us

In the ancient language of Geez, Halziz means "Seashell" a symbol of organic intelligence, layered complexity, and hidden strength. Today, Halziz is Ethiopia’s premier advanced technology company The Monolithic Matrix of Tech, engineering the intelligent infrastructure that will power Africa’s digital future and achieve #DIGITALETHIOPIA2030.

Our Mission

To make Africa proud and achieve Digital Ethiopia 2030 architecting, building, and securing the foundational technology systems that enable Ethiopia’s digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted technology architect a sovereign force in intelligent infrastructure, cybersecurity, and autonomous systems that empowers every Ethiopian and African to thrive in the digital age.
